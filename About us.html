WHO ARE WE?


We are an organisation that rescues animals from streets and open our doors to owners who want to relieve themselves of pets. We accept all animals and ensure they undergo treatment. for this we have the best vetinarys who are patient and knowlegdable in matters animal health.

We are licensed to allow families to adopt these animals after a thorough background check up. aftwerwards we assist the families in the birthing of the animals and monthly checkups.
From their adoptions, we are able to sustain the animals by paying for elecrticity bills, food, and other supplies needed. We encourage donations fro foreign and local wellwishers too.
This website will help you navigate to all supplies you may need to purchase for your pets. We will list the items, their descriptions and their prices. Customers will be able to order them online and receive the bought product in a matter of days. They will receive written advisory articles on how to keep various pets.

All are welcome!
